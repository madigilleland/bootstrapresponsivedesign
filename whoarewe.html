Who Are We?

We are JMU's Premier Christian Upper Voices A Cappella Group!

But really that's just a fancy introduction we use.

Who are we really?

We are a group of women who love singing, loving, and praising God together! Apart from that, we honestly just love each other so deeply. You can find us anywhere on campus hanging out together. We hope to share with our community the love of God through our music.
